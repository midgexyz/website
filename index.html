Beginner Beast Chess Strategy

Climb from 300 to 800+ Rapid Rating with Focused Fundamentals

Core Strategy

  • Avoid blunders.
  • Punish hanging pieces.
  • Use one reliable setup every game.
  • Trade when ahead and simplify.

Opening Setup

As White (London-style setup):
  • 1. d4
  • 2. Nf3
  • 3. Bf4
  • 4. e3
  • 5. Bd3
  • 6. c3
  • 7. Nbd2
  • 8. O-O
As Black vs 1.e4:
  • 1... e5
  • 2... Nf6
  • 3... Bc5
As Black vs 1.d4:
  • 1... d5
  • 2... Nf6
  • 3... Bf5 or e6
